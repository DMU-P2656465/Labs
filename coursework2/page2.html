    • Cirencester

    Cirencester is the Capital of the Cotswolds, and a famous Roman built town, with still some roads still in tact. As well as this, there is still an amphitheatre in Cirencester, it's covered in grass but it's clear what it used to be! The video linked below gives a great background history to the town and surrounding areas.

    This is Anne Boleyn's Cup from the 1500's, which was orginally given to her Daughter Elizabeth I, from her, given to Dr Masters, who lived near the area and so gifted it to St John's Baptist Church in the centre of Cirencester, and has been there ever since, perfectly preserved.
