Fionn Whitehead is a 24 year old English actor, born in Richard Upon Thames, in Surrey, who plays Tommy in the 2017 film, Dunkirk. Whitehead had always been academic, achieiving 12 passes at A/A* level, then going on to college and entering the National Youth Theatre's summer course. Before this role, Fionn had played in an ITV mini-series named HIM, which was aired in 2016, whilst he was continuing to be a member of the Youth Theatre. When Dunkirk was released, Fionn was only 19, after just finishing the shooting of The Children Act, along side Emma Thompson and Stanley Tucci. An interview with Fionn can be played below. Harry Styles is a 27 year old English actor, born in Worcestershire, England. Originally in the music industry, Styles had a 7 year music career, starting in a boyband called One Direction who were formed on the X Factor in 2010, and finishing 3rd in the competition. After the band announced their hiatus in 2015, Styles went on his own music adventure before landing his first acting role playing Alex in Dunkirk, to do along side music. The film topped the USA Box office in it's first weekend of release and continued to be one of the highest grossing films of Summer 2017. Aneurin Barnard is a Welsh actor, born in 1987. He is known for doing many musicals and theatre shows, as well as taking part in the occasional film. Barnard originated the character of Melchior Gabor in Spring Awakening, the London West End hit musical. He plays Gibson in the 2017 film Dunkirk, and is also known for his work in Cilla (2014). When he was younger, he used to pretend he was playing different character's like cowboys, and used to daydream about acting and being in films. Aneurin has also discussed that he carries out his own stunts when acting, as he believes that it is part of the job, and is something he is very passionate about.
