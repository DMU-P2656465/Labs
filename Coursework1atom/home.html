Dunkirk is an action-thriller film based on the historical event that occoured in 1940, during the second world war. This film ecapsulates the action and the horrifying realities of the traumatic event, where hundreds of thousands of allies are rescued from the Nazis near the beaches of Dunkirk, France, after Nazi Germany took over. In this film, we're taken on a terrifying journey with characters Tommy, Alex, Gibson, their peers and fellow British Navy force to sacrifice themselves in order to rescue their allies from danger.
